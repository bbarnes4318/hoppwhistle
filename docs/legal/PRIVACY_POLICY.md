## Introduction

Hopwhistle ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our telephony platform and services (the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, company name, billing information
- **Usage Data:** Call logs, call recordings, transcripts, campaign data, analytics
- **Configuration Data:** Phone numbers, routing rules, flows, integrations
- **Support Communications:** Information provided when contacting support

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
- **Usage Analytics:** Feature usage, performance metrics, error logs
- **Call Metadata:** Call duration, timestamps, call routing information, costs

### Information from Third Parties

- **Telephony Providers:** Call data, phone number information, carrier data
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction information, payment methods
- **Integration Partners:** Data from connected services (CRM, analytics, etc.)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and Maintain the Service**
   - Process and route calls
   - Manage phone numbers and campaigns
   - Generate invoices and process payments
   - Provide customer support

2. **Improve and Personalize the Service**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Develop new features
   - Customize user experience

3. **Communicate with You**
   - Send service-related notifications
   - Respond to support requests
   - Send marketing communications (with consent)

4. **Comply with Legal Obligations**
   - Comply with applicable laws and regulations
   - Respond to legal requests
   - Enforce our terms of service

5. **Security and Fraud Prevention**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Protect against security threats
   - Ensure platform security
