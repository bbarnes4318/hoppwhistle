# Legal & Compliance Documentation

This directory contains legal and compliance templates for the Hopwhistle platform.

## Documents

- **[Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md)** - Privacy policy template covering data collection, use, and user rights
- **[Data Retention Policy](./DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md)** - Policy outlining data retention periods and deletion procedures
- **[Call Recording Disclosure Guide](./CALL_RECORDING_DISCLOSURE.md)** - Informational guide on call recording requirements by U.S. state
- **[Data Processing Agreement (DPA)](./DPA_TEMPLATE.md)** - Template DPA for B2B customers (GDPR/CCPA compliant)
- **[Terms of Service](./TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md)** - Terms of service boilerplate

## Important Notes

⚠️ **These are template documents and do not constitute legal advice.**

Before using these documents:

1. **Review with Legal Counsel:** All documents should be reviewed and customized by qualified legal counsel
2. **Customize for Your Jurisdiction:** Laws vary by jurisdiction - ensure compliance with local regulations
3. **Update Placeholders:** Replace all placeholders (e.g., [Date], [Your Company Address]) with actual information
4. **Industry-Specific Requirements:** Consider industry-specific regulations (HIPAA, FINRA, etc.)
5. **Regular Updates:** Legal requirements change - review and update documents regularly
